Under choose your installer, you'll notice copies for Windows®, Mac® and GNU/Linux™. In most cases, the website will detect what operating system you are running and have the correct version selected.

You can also choose your version of python™. For this course I recommend version 3.4 or higher.

You also need to choose between 32-bit (for older computers) and 64-bit (most modern computers).

If you click on the link for *Python Packages* and choose the tab for version you install, you see a list of all the extention libraries / modules that gets added to the base python™. Here we have the true power of python™.
